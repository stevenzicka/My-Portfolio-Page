More About Me

I'm a Civil Engineer out of Oregon State University (Go Beavs!!) looking to make a transition into software development. I'm currently in school (at OSU again), and set to graduate spring of 2023 with a bachelor's degree in Computer Science!

I have been self studying Computer Science since 2019 and finally decided to take the leap into a full time program at the end of 2021. Since then, I have been working full time as a Civil Engineer and attending school part time in my off-hours. Any freetime I get these days is spent outside in nature or cooking for my girlfriend (and dog).

My coding journey didn't start there though. In 2009, I took a course at DigiPen in Redmond, WA over the summer. This was my first exposure to programming and I absolutely loved it! After that I knew I wanted to be a developer. Unfortunately, life had different plans for me. After my house burned down in 2011, my life's trajectory changed course. I decided that losing everything would allow me to have a fresh start and leave my past behind me. Soon after, I decided to pursue Civil Engineering inspired from seeing the progress of my home being rebuilt.

It wasn't until about a year after my graduation I started to look at some graduate programs and noticed one of them had a course that included some Python. So I went online and started learning. I took some courses, bought some programming books, and never looked back.

Today my passion for programming is stronger than ever!
